About Us

With over 50 years of experience, J&R Tec stands for more than just a vehicle mechanic service; we're your trusted partner on the road. Located between Wittedrift and the Greater Plettenberg Bay, our operational range extends from Knysna to The Crags, where we specialize in comprehensive vehicle repair and maintenance services.
Although our regular service area is within these regions, exceptions can be made to meet your needs in emergency situations.

From minor tune-ups to major overhauls, we offer reliable and efficient solutions for all your automotive needs. We offer roadside and onsite assistance when possible. Prepping for a roadworthy test? Count on us to get your car in tip-top shape, meeting all the regulatory requirements.

Services

General Vehicle Services

  • Comprehensive service and repairs for various vehicle brands, including cars, bakkies, trucks, and construction machinery.

  • Expertise in small hydraulic repairs for construction machines, including pipe repair and replacement.

  • Skilled in auto repairs and electric replacements, covering essentials like globes, starters, alternators, and more.

  • Thorough roadworthy checks for all road-going vehicles, ensuring compliance with regulatory requirements.

  • Service and repairs for smallplant, generators and water pumps. Repairs on waterpumps, including seal and bearing replacements.

Recoveries

  • Flexible recovery services encompassing towing and minor roadside repairs.

  • Prompt assistance available in Plettenberg Bay and surrounding areas.

  • Tailored recovery solutions ensuring swift and safe return to your journey.

  • Trusted partner for various recovery scenarios, minimizing journey disruptions.

On-site Repairs

  • Efficient on-site repair services for urgent needs, available across Plettenberg Bay, Knysna and The Crags areas.

  • Swift solutions for a range of issues from gearbox problems to electrical repairs, minimizing vehicle downtime.

  • We are equipped to handle minor to complex repairs, ensuring a quick return to optimal vehicle condition.

  • High-quality repair solutions, prioritizing your safety and convenience.

In-Shop Repairs

  • Comprehensive in-shop repair services covering a wide spectrum from engine overhauls to brake repairs.

  • Specialized maintenance, including roadworthy checks for all vehicle types, and repair services for small plant like generators and water pumps.

  • Small hydraulic repairs on most construction machines, including repairs on or replacement of pipes.

  • Minor auto electrical repairs and replacements such as globes, starters, alternators, etc.

  • We deliver high-quality service and reliable solutions, ensuring your vehicle or machinery is maintained to the highest standard.
